The simple answer: what to run now

Good Terror Zone

Run it for the full 30-minute window. Best examples for you: Chaos Sanctuary, Worldstone Keep/Baal, Catacombs/Andariel, Durance/Mephisto, Travincal, and Cows.

Bad Terror Zone

Run Chaos Sanctuary. It is your best repeatable all-purpose route without Enigma: experience, elite packs, runes, bases, charms, and Diablo.

Specific need

Cows for bases/gems; Andy/Meph for uniques; Countess for low-mid runes and Terror keys; Summoner/Nihlathak for the other keys.

Your recommended default: good Hell Terror Zone → Chaos Sanctuary → one targeted route only when you need something specific. Avoid spending an entire session cycling every boss; specialization makes each run more efficient.
Current-client note: Terror Zones now rotate every 30 minutes. In the 2026 client, Hell Terror Zones can also spawn Heralds; kill them when safe, but do not weaken your normal build solely to hunt them.

Gems, jewels, and crafting materials

Pick up every flawless gem

  • Perfect Amethyst: caster amulets and weapon socket recipe.
  • Perfect Ruby: blood crafts and shield socket recipe.
  • Perfect Topaz: armor socket recipe and MF socketing.
  • Any Perfect Gem: magic charm rerolls.
  • Perfect Skull: rare-item reroll recipes and charm rerolls.

Keep ordinary jewels

Crafted items consume a jewel of any quality. Also inspect every jewel before using it: 15% IAS, high enhanced damage, all resistance, and useful combinations can be valuable.

Magic Find strategy for your character

Runes and base-item selection do not benefit from Magic Find. MF increases the chance that eligible equipment upgrades in quality to magic, rare, set, or unique. Do not weaken the character when hunting bases or runes.

Progression / dangerous TZ

Vipermagi, defensive circlet, capped resistances, ≥75 FCR (preferably 125), ≥86 FHR.

Balanced farm

Aim for roughly 100–200 MF while preserving clear speed. Add MF charms, Nagelring, or the Topaz crown one piece at a time.

Boss swap

Use your 3× Perfect Topaz crown, 29% MF amulet, Nagelring, or Skullder’s only when the boss remains fast and safe.

Breakpoint warning: if your current Vipermagi setup is approximately 114 FCR, swapping one 10-FCR ring for Nagelring leaves about 104—still the 75 breakpoint. Adding 20-FCR gloves would then produce about 124, which is still not 125. Always verify the exact total rather than assuming.

Fast drop-decision guide

White / gray item

  1. Is it ethereal?
  2. Is it elite or a known low-requirement caster base?
  3. Does it have the exact socket count for a valuable runeword?
  4. For Paladin shields: does it have 35–45 all resistance?
  5. For merc armor: can the merc actually meet the Strength requirement?

Rare / magic / unique item

  1. Does it add +skills, FCR, life, resistances, MF, leech, IAS, Crushing Blow, or damage reduction?
  2. Does it solve a breakpoint?
  3. Is it useful for an Uber Smiter or common alt?
  4. Is the roll near perfect?
  5. When uncertain, stash one copy and ask before vendoring.

For bases, a correct socket count is often more important than defense. For premium runewords, the expensive rune—not the base—is the resource you should protect.
